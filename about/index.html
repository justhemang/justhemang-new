THE WORK

I'm Hemang Luthra — a freelance video editor, frontend developer, and creative director from Delhi, India. I've been editing videos since 2021, working across YouTube content, reels, short films, and client projects. I specialize in storytelling-driven cuts, motion graphics, color grading, and kinetic typography. Beyond video, I build immersive web experiences with 6+ years of frontend development under my belt. As President of OPCODE, my school's tech club, I co-hosted and judged our annual tech fest XENIUM. I'm also the founder of Kaelor Media, where I bring together video production and digital design under one roof. Every frame I edit, every line of code I write — it's all about making something that moves people.
